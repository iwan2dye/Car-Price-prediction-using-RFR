Welcome to Value my car !

"Value My Car" is a simple website that uses python and machine learning in it's core to predicts/estimate the market value (in US$) of your used car. This problem involves predicting the price of a automotive vehicle based on various features and characteristics, such as the make and model, year, mileage, City, and additional factors like fuel type that can influence its current worth.
For individuals looking to sell or buy a used car, knowing the accurate market value is crucial. It empowers consumers by ensuring that they receive a fair price for their vehicle or make informed purchasing decisions. This can prevent both overpayment and undervaluation.
