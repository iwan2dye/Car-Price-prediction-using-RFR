What Algorihtm is used?

"Value My Car" uses Random Forest Regressor at its core to predict the price of the car and trained on a dataset collected from Kaggle

Why?

The Reason for using abc at its core to make predictions is simple. During our model building we used multiple algorithmns and measured, recorded and compared their preformance, out of which abc preformed the best and after hyperparameter-tuning the model seems to work better.
